Charles Darwin?

查尔斯 达尔文？

Charles Darwin is known to be the father of the evolution theory in public, but is he really the first person to post this theory? Or is he the only person at the time to do that?

众所周知，查尔斯-达尔文是进化论之父，但他真的是第一个发表这一理论的人吗？或者说他是当时唯一一个这样做的人？

The Pioneer 先驱者

----There are a lot of discussion about which biologist was the first to realize that species evolve by natural selection. The biologist Charles Darwin is the one who takes this title most of the time becuase he developed and first published this theory.

----关于哪位生物学家是第一个意识到物种通过自然选择进化的人有很多讨论。 生物学家查尔斯-达尔文在大多数情况下是这个称号的获得者，因为他提出并首次发表了这个理论。

----Interestingly, another biologist Alfred Wallace wrote a letter to Darwin suggesting similar ideas. This encouraged Darwin to post his astonishing findings that he orginally fear to publish because of how pioneering it is. While someone may argue that there are other biologists who also have this idea at the time, it is important to note that Darwin already conducted a 20 years research on this topic.

----有趣的是，另一位生物学家阿尔弗雷德-华莱士给达尔文写了一封信，提出了类似的想法。这鼓励了达尔文发表了他惊人的发现，而他最初是害怕发表这些发现的， 因为它是如此的领先。虽然有人会争辩说当时还有其他生物学家也有这种想法, 但是要知道达尔文已经对这个话题进行了20年的研究。

Charles Darwin 查尔斯 达尔文

----Charles Robert Darwin was an English naturalist, geologist and biologist, best known for his contributions to evolutionary biology. His proposition that all species of life have descended from common ancestors is now widely accepted and considered a fundamental concept in science. In a joint publication with Alfred Russel Wallace, he introduced his scientific theory that this branching pattern of evolution resulted from a process that he called natural selection, in which the struggle for existence has a similar effect to the artificial selection involved in selective breeding. Darwin has been described as one of the most influential figures in human history,and he was honoured by burial in Westminster Abbey.

----查尔斯-罗伯特-达尔文是英国自然学家、地质学家和生物学家，因其对进化生物学的贡献而最为知名。他提出的所有生命物种都是由共同的祖先演化而来， 这一主张现在已被广泛接受，并被认为是科学中的一个基本概念。在与阿尔弗雷德-拉塞尔-华莱士的联合出版物中，他提出了自己的科学理论， 即这种进化的分支模式是由他称之为自然选择的过程造成的。他称之为自然选择的过程，在这个过程中，为生存而进行的斗争具有类似于人工选择的效果。 达尔文被描述为人类历史上最有影响力的人物之一，他被荣幸地安葬在威斯敏斯特教堂。

----Darwin published his theory of evolution with compelling evidence in his 1859 book On the Origin of Species. By the 1870s, the scientific community and a majority of the educated public had accepted evolution as a fact. However, many favoured competing explanations that gave only a minor role to natural selection, and it was not until the emergence of the modern evolutionary synthesis from the 1930s to the 1950s that a broad consensus developed in which natural selection was the basic mechanism of evolution. Darwin's scientific discovery is the unifying theory of the life sciences, explaining the diversity of life.

----达尔文在其1859年出版的《物种起源》一书中发表了具有令人信服的证据的进化论。到19世纪70年代，科学界和大多数受过教育的公众已经接受了进化论这一事实。 然而，许多人偏爱那些只给自然选择一个小角色的竞争性解释，直到20世纪30年代到50年代现代进化论综合的出现，才形成一个广泛的共识，即自然选择是进化的基本机制。 达尔文的科学发现是生命科学的统一理论，解释了生命的多样性。

----Darwin's early interest in nature led him to neglect his medical education at the University of Edinburgh; instead, he helped to investigate marine invertebrates. Studies at the University of Cambridge (Christ's College) encouraged his passion for natural science. His five-year voyage on HMS Beagle established him as an eminent geologist whose observations and theories supported Charles Lyell's conception of gradual geological change, and publication of his journal of the voyage made him famous as a popular author.

----达尔文早期对自然的兴趣使他忽视了在爱丁堡大学的医学教育；相反，他帮助研究海洋无脊椎动物。在剑桥大学（基督学院）的学习鼓励了他对自然科学的热情。 他在贝格尔号上为期五年的航行使他成为一名杰出的地质学家。他的观察和理论支持了查尔斯-莱尔的渐进式地质变化概念。他的航行日志的出版使他成为著名的通俗作家。

----Puzzled by the geographical distribution of wildlife and fossils he collected on the voyage, Darwin began detailed investigations and in 1838 conceived his theory of natural selection. Although he discussed his ideas with several naturalists, he needed time for extensive research, and his geological work had priority. He was writing up his theory in 1858 when Alfred Russel Wallace sent him an essay that described the same idea, prompting immediate joint publication of both their theories. Darwin's work established evolutionary descent with modification as the dominant scientific explanation of diversification in nature. In 1871 he examined human evolution and sexual selection in The Descent of Man, and Selection in Relation to Sex, followed by The Expression of the Emotions in Man and Animals (1872). His research on plants was published in a series of books, and in his final book, The Formation of Vegetable Mould, through the Actions of Worms (1881), he examined earthworms and their effect on soil.

----达尔文对他在航行中收集到的野生动物和化石的地理分布感到困惑，开始进行详细的调查。并在1838年构思了他的自然选择理论。尽管他与几位自然科学家讨论了他的想法， 但他需要时间进行广泛的研究，而他的地质工作具有优先权。1858年，他正在写他的理论时，阿尔弗雷德-拉塞尔-华莱士给他寄来了一篇文章， 描述了同样的理论促使他们立即联合出版了这一理论。达尔文的工作确立了 达尔文的工作确立了进化的后裔，作为对自然界多样性的主要科学解释。 1871年，他在《进化论》中研究了人类 1871年，他在《人的后裔》和《与性别有关的选择》中研究了人类的进化和性选择，随后又发表了 《人和动物的情感表达》（1872年）。他对植物的研究发表在一系列书中，在他的最后一本书《植物霉菌的形成》 （The Formation of Vegetable（1881年）中，他研究了蚯蚓及其对土壤的影响。
