Animalia 动物界

Most of the kingdoms in the eukaryotes domain are what most people are familiar with. The current six kingdom under this domain are chromoalveolata, rhizaria, excavata, animalia, plantae, and fungi. You are probably not very familiar with the first three kingdom which represent some very important but small eukaryotic organisms. The last three should be familiar to you even by their name: animals, plants, and mushrooms (representative). In this section, I am going to comment on 7 large phyla under the kingdom of animalia and I will talk a little about their characteristics so you might be able to distinguish some in the future.

真核生物中的界大部分都是我们熟知的生物。目前这个领域下的六个王国是囊泡藻界, 有孔虫界, 古虫界, 动物界, 植物界和真菌界。 你可能对前三个王国不是很熟悉 它们代表了一些非常重要但规模较小的真核生物体。后三者你应该很熟悉，甚至从它们的名字就可以看出：动物。植物和蘑菇（代表）。 在本节中，我将对动物界下的7个大门类进行评论。我将谈一谈它们的特点，这样你就可以在将来区分一些生活中的生物了。

Porifera 海绵动物门

Think of a sponge for now. Do you know what that is? Scientist were once confused by their unique nature before and categorize them into plants. However, they later found out they have more of an animal characteristic than plants.To distiguish them, you can simply just think of a sponge. But you can also distinguish them with characteristics below.

现在先想一想海绵。你知道那是什么吗？科学家们曾经被它们的独特性质所迷惑，把它们归类为植物。然而，他们后来发现，它们比植物更具有动物的特征。 为了区分它们，你可以简单地认为是海绵。但你也可以通过以下特征来区分它们。

----They have no clear symmetry

----他们通常不对称

----They normally attched to a surface

----他们通常附着一个表面上

----They have pores through body

----他们身体里有贯穿的孔洞

----They have no mouth or anus

----他们没有嘴巴和排泄孔

Cnidaria 刺胞动物门

Animals in Cnidaria are more motional then the Porifera. A lot of very beautiful creatures live in this phylum. While all animals in this phylum live in water this is not a characteristic specific to them. Think of a jellyfish and a sea anemone both in the Cnidaria phyla. They seems to be quite different at first isn't it. But when you know their common characteristics, you should be able to distinguish them from others.

在刺胞类动物中，动物的运动性比多孔类更强。许多非常美丽的生物生活在这个门类中。虽然 这个门类的所有动物都生活在水中，这并不是它们特有的特征。 想想看，水母和海葵都属于刺胞动物门。它们一开始似乎很不一样，不是吗？但是，当你知道它们的共同特征时，你就应该能够把它们与其他动物区分开来。

----They are radically symmetric (basically they look like a circle when you look from above or under)

----他们径向对称（简单来说从他们的上方或下方看他们是个圆圈）

----They have tentacles

----他们有触手

----They have stinging cells (they can sting!)

----他们有刺细胞（他们可以刺伤你！）

----They have mouth but no anus

----他们有嘴巴但没有排泄孔

Platyhelminths 扁形动物门

If you ever saw a picture of a flat worm, this is the phylum it belongs to. A picture of it is right below. It may seem to you as a worm or something, but it is different. It is a lot smaller and very very flat. In fact, this is why they are named this way.

如果你曾经看到过扁形虫的图片，这就是它所属的门类。下面是它的照片。在你看来，它可能是一种蠕虫或什么，但它是不同的。 它要小得多，而且非常非常扁平。事实上，这就是它们被这样命名的原因。

----They are bilaterally symmetric (they are symmetric)

----他们两侧对称

----They have flat body

----他们的身体很扁

----They are not segmented(unlike earth worm or insect)

----他们的身体没有分段（不像蚯蚓和其他一些昆虫）

----They have mouth but no anus

----他们有嘴巴但没有排泄孔

Annelida 环节动物门

One animal from Annelida that is most familiar to you might be earth worm. You might wonder if caterpillars are one of them, they are not because they eventually turn into moths and butterflies. Let's look at their characteristics.

你最熟悉的Annelida中的一种动物可能是蚯蚓。你可能想知道毛毛虫是否是其中之一。它们不是，因为它们最终会变成蛾子和蝴蝶。让我们来看看它们的特点吧。

----They are bilaterally symmetric (they are symmetric)

----他们两侧对称

----They often have bristles

----他们通常有刚毛

----They are segmented

----他们的身体是分段的

----They have mouth and anus

----他们有嘴巴和排泄孔

Arthropoda 节肢动物门

Arthropoda includes all insects and some others such as crabs, and spiders. One fact is that the arthropada phylum has the largest numbers of organisms in all phyla of animalia kingdom.

节肢动物门包括所有的昆虫和一些其他动物，如螃蟹和蜘蛛。一个事实是，节肢动物门拥有动物界所有门类中数量最多的生物。是动物界所有门类中数量最多的生物。

----They are bilaterally symmetric (they are symmetric)

----他们两侧对称

----They have exoskeleton (if you ever touch a insect or even a crab, you may notice they have a hard shell. That is the exo skeleton)

----他们的有外骨骼（如果你曾经触摸过昆虫或甚至螃蟹，你可能会注意到它们有一个硬壳。这就是外骨骼）

----They are segmented

----他们的身体有分段

----They have mouth and anus

----他们有嘴巴和排泄孔

----They have jointed appendages (they have extra legs)

----他们拥有有关节的附肢（他们有多余的腿）

Mollusca 软体动物门

You can start to think of slimy things for this one. The reason you feel that they are slimy is because they don't have skeleton. So, what do they often do to protect themselves is that they make a shell. Snails is the most famous of them.

对于这个问题，你可以开始想一些粘糊糊的东西。你觉得它们黏糊糊的原因是它们没有骨架。所以它们经常形成一个壳来保护自己。蜗牛是其中最著名的。

----They have muscular foot and mantle

----他们拥有有肌肉的足部和外层

----They often have a shell

----他们通常有个壳

----They have segmentation that is not visible

----他们的身体有不可见的分段

----They have mouth and anus

----他们有嘴巴和排泄孔

Chordata 脊索动物门

Chordata may sound unknown to you but all the animals within it are the ones you know the best. Almost all the larger animals are from this phylum including us. The biggest different this group have is that they have a notochord. This structure allow these animals to have larger weight since it can support the weight. Think of your own backbone. All mammals have this structure in them.

脊索动物听起来可能不为人知，但其中的所有动物都是你最熟悉的。几乎所有的大型动物都来自这个门类包括我们。这个门类最大的不同是它们有一根脊索。 这种结构使这些动物 有更大的重量，因为它可以支撑重量。想想你自己的脊柱。所有哺乳动物都有这种结构。

----They have a notochord

----他们拥有脊索

----They have a dorsal nerve chord

----他们拥有背神经管

----They have pharyngeal gill slits (implies that we evolve from water living creatures.)

----他们拥有咽部鳃裂（暗示我们是从水生生物进化而来的）

----They have post-anal tail (we used to have a tail too)

----他们有肛后尾（我们曾经是有尾巴的，现在退化了）

The Vertebrates 脊椎动物亚门

Now you know the different levels in taxonomy, you might wonder is all grouping strictly restricted to these different levels. The answer is no. There are sub-groups under each larger level for organisms holding similar characteristics but not small enough to be put into the next smaller level. The vertebrates is one of such groups of organism being a sub-phylum of the phylum of chordata.

现在你知道了分类学中的不同层次，你可能想知道所有的分组是否都严格限制在这些不同的层次上。答案是否定的。 在每一个较大的层次下都有一些子群，供具有类似特征但又不够小的生物体归入下一个较小的层次。脊椎动物亚门就是这样一群生物被归入了脊索动物门下的亚门
