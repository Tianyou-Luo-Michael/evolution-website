进化论祖师爷和后人的分类

Taxonomy with Knowledge of Evolution

进化论之后的分类

Human started to classify organisms from very early time in the history, but the ways of classification has never stay constant due new discoveries and technologies. However, after the theory of evolution, classification became clearer since scientist became more knowledgeable with how organisms evolve.

人类在历史上很早就开始对生物进行分类，但由于新的发现和技术的出现，分类的方法从未保持不变。然而，在进化论提出后， 由于科学家对生物如何进化有了更多的了解，分类也变得更加清晰了。

Hierarchy of taxa 分类阶元

Hierarchy of taxa is basically the different levels of category where organisms can be placed in. There are eight levels of hierarchy in total. Starting from the smallest: species, genus, family, order, class, phylum, kingdom, and domain. The reason that there are this many levels is because of the huge amount of organisms existed in our world. Starting from the very bottom, species is the smallest posible level to identify an organism. Human or sapiens are the species that we belong to. Moving up a level is the genus which is homo for human. Currently, we are the only species existing in the genus of homo right now. Other species in the homo genus already extinct. Going further up is the family and order. I am going to skip this to and go right to class. A class will become more familiar to you such as birds and mammals. The phylum above further increase the range of what each contain such as angiospermophytes including all flowering plants. Going further up is the kingdom which include ones such as animalia and plantae. The last level of domain include only three domain: Archaea, Eubacteria, and Eukaryotes. It is interesting to note that virus is not considered as a living organisms anymore. It used to be categorized into prokaryotes with bacterias.

分类阶元是指生物体可以被归入的不同级别的类别。层次结构总共有八个级别。从最小的开始：种、属、科、目、类、门、界和域。 之所以有这么多层次是因为我们的世界存在着大量的生物体。从最底层开始，种是识别一个生物体的最小可能的层次。人类或智人是我们所属的种。 再往上一级是属，也就是人类的属。目前，我们是人属中唯一存在的物种。人属中的其他物种已经灭绝了。再往上走就是科和目。我打算跳过这一点直接到科的那一层。 这一类会让你更熟悉，如鸟类和哺乳动物。上面的门进一步增加了各自包含的范围如被子植物包括所有会开花的的植物。再往上走就是王国包括动物界和植物界等。 最后一级的域只包括三个域。古细菌、真细菌和真核生物。有意思的是病毒已经不再被科学家们视为活着的生物了。
