Evidence of Evolution(specific)

实例中的进化论依据

Evidence in general may help us understand how the evolution theory is supported overall. But the specific examples are the ones that can help us to understand deeper.

宏观的进化论依据可以让我们大概了解支持进化论方法，但是单个中的实例证据才能让我们更好的理解进化论

Example of Melanism in Pepered Moth 黑化桦尺蠖的实例

----Industrialization has affected out environment mostly in a bad way. But the response of the peppered moth to the environment demonstrated the concept of natural selection and evolution in explicit way.

工业化对环境的影响大多是不好的。但飞蛾对环境的反应明确地描绘了自然选择和进化的概念。

----1. 斑纹桦尺蛾在夜间飞行，交配和繁殖。

----1. Peppered moth fly at night to try to mate and reproduce.

----1. 斑纹桦尺蛾在夜间飞行，交配和繁殖。

----2. During the day they rest on the branches of trees.

----2. 白天它们在树枝上休息。

----3. In unpolluted areas tree branches are covered in pale-colored lichens, so peppered moth are well hidden against them.

----3. 在没有污染的地区，树枝上覆盖着浅色的地衣，所以斑纹桦尺蛾在树枝上可以很好地隐藏。

----4. The pollution killed the lichens on the tree branches and the soot from coal burning blackens them.

----4. 污染杀死了树枝上的地衣而后燃煤产生的烟尘使它们变黑。

----5. Melanic moth are well hidden against dark tree branches.

----5. 黑化桦尺蛾在深色的树枝上隐藏得很好。

----6. Birds and other animals that hunt in daylight catch the moths if they find them.

----6.鸟类和其他在白天捕食的动物如果发现了这些飞蛾就会抓到它们。

----7. At places being polluted by industrial revolution the peppered variety were mostly found and eaten, while the melanic ones survived and reproduced. Therefore, this cause the peppered moth evolved to be melanic moth.

----7.在被工业革命污染的地方，斑纹桦尺蛾大多被发现并被吃掉，而黑化的桦尺蛾则存活并繁殖。因此，这就导致了斑纹桦尺飞蛾进化为黑色桦尺飞蛾。
