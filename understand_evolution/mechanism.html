Understanding Evolution

理解进化论

Mechanism of Evolution

进化的机制

The process of evolution is exactly the process of natural selection.

进化的本质就是自然选择

Natural Selection 自然选择

The process of evolution may sounds extremely complicated at first. It is normal to feel this way since how would you expect a random weird looking organism living in the ocean milliones years ago to be our ancestor? However, when you realize that the complete process of a full evolution are countless occasions of natrual selection, it becomes easier to understand the difference in two species of same ancestry. Our ancestor did not become as smart as us in one night, but in numerous generations. In several generations, there might be one individual smarter than the others and is able to survive better. His or her offspring are therefore smarter. This pattern continues on until today.

进化的过程一开始可能听起来非常复杂。有这种感觉是正常的，因为你怎么会想到一个随机的、看起来很奇怪的海洋生物体是我们的祖先？ 然而当你意识到一个完整的进化过程是无数次的自然选择后你就能更好地理解同祖先物种存在在的差距了。我们的祖先并不是在一夜之间 变得像我们一样聪明而是在无数个世代中逐渐演变成现在的样子。在几代人中，可能有一个人 比其他人更聪明，能够更好地生存。 因此，他或她的后代也更聪明。这种模式如此一直持续到今天。

The concept of natural selection is easy to understand comparing to evolution because of its shorter time lapse and stong logic. THe whole process is basically a series of reasonable deduction and obsevation.

与进化论相比，自然选择的概念很容易理解，因为它的时间间隔较短，逻辑性强。整个过程基本上是一系列合理的推理和观察。

----1. Species tend to produce more offspring than the environment can support.

----1. 物种倾向于生产比环境所能支持的更多的后代。

----2. Some survive and some die during competition for living.

----2. 在生存竞争中，有的生存，有的死亡。

----3. In natural populations there is variation between the individuals.

----3. 在自然种群中，个体之间存在差异。

----4. Some individual adapted better than others. An adaptation is a characteristic that makes an individual suited to its environment and way of life.

----4. 有些个体比其他个体适应得更好。适应性是一种使个体更适合其环境和生活方式的特征。

----5. Once that are better adapted tend to survive and produce more offspring. Those who adapted less well tend to die and therefore produce less offspring. Therefore each generation contain more offspring of better adapted parents than offspring of less well adapted parents.

----5. 适应性较好的个体往往能存活下来并产生更多的后代。那些适应性较差的个体往往会死亡，因此产生的后代也较少。因此，每一代人中，适应性较好的父母的后代比适应性较差的父母的后代多。

----6. Individuals that reproduce pass on their characteristics to their offspring.

----6. 繁殖的个体将其特征传给其后代。

----7. The existence of charateristic that make individuals better survive and adapt increases and The existence of charateristic that make individuals less posible to survive and adapt decreases. Therefore species change and become better adapted.

----7. 能让个体更好地生存和适应的特性的存在会增加，使个体不太可能生存和适应的特性的存在会减少。 因此，物种会发生变化，变得更加适应周边环境。
